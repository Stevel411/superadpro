# SuperAdPro — Business Overview & Financial Breakdown
**Last updated: 3 April 2026**

---

## 1. Platform Overview

SuperAdPro is a multi-tool SaaS platform targeting network marketers and digital product sellers. The platform combines affiliate/referral income streams, AI creative tools, a Watch-to-Earn grid engine, link tools, and a course marketplace.

**Live URL:** https://www.superadpro.com
**Stack:** FastAPI/Python backend, React/Vite frontend, PostgreSQL, Railway
**Payment:** USDT/USDC (Polygon) via direct wallet + NOWPayments (350+ cryptos)

### 2.5 Pay It Forward Gift Vouchers

Members pay $20 from their wallet to gift a Basic membership to someone new.

- Gifter pays $20 → creates a voucher link
- Recipient joins free → membership activates immediately
- Gifter becomes the sponsor → earns referral commissions
- When recipient earns $20+, prompted to pay it forward
- Chain tracking links each gift to its parent (generations)

**Platform revenue:** $20 per gift (same as Basic membership)
**Commission:** Normal sponsor commission applies ($10)
**Net to platform:** $10 per gift

## 3. Commission Structure — What Affiliates Earn

### 3.1 Membership Referral Commissions

When someone refers a new member:
- Basic referral: **$10** (50% of $20)
- Pro referral: **$17.50** (50% of $35)

Paid instantly to the referring member's wallet.

### 3.2 Campaign Tier Commissions (8×8 Income Grid)

**Direct Sponsor Commission (40%):**
When your personal referral activates a tier, you earn 40% of the tier price.
- T1: $8, T2: $20, T3: $40, T4: $80, T5: $160, T6: $240, T7: $320, T8: $400

**Uni-Level Commissions (6.25% per level × 8 levels):**
You earn 6.25% of every tier activation in your downline, 8 levels deep.
This equals 50% of the tier price distributed across the 8 upline levels.

**Grid Completion Bonus (5% pool):**
5% of every tier activation funds the bonus pool. When your 8×8 grid fills all 64 positions, you receive the accumulated bonus.
- T1: $64, T2: $160, T3: $320, T4: $640, T5: $1,280, T6: $1,920, T7: $2,560, T8: $3,200

### 3.3 SuperScene Sponsor Micro-Commissions

When someone in your downline uses SuperScene credits:
- **$0.025 per credit used**
- Example: Referral generates a 10-second Kling 3.0 video (6 credits) → you earn $0.15
- These accumulate and can add up significantly with active users
