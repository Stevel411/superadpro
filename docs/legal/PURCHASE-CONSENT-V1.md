  SuperAdPro company treasury.

This immediate activation and immediate commission payout is the
heart of how the platform works. It's also why we cannot refund

## 3. Why we cannot offer refunds on request

We have built SuperAdPro on a "real-time payout" compensation model.
The moment your payment confirms:

- A portion goes to **your sponsor's wallet** as a direct commission.
- Smaller portions go to **multiple uplines' wallets** through the
  uni-level structure.
- For Grid/Nexus purchases, contributions also feed the **completion
  bonus pool**, which can pay out to a third party at any moment.
- The remainder goes to the **company treasury** to fund platform
  operations.

Once those amounts are in third parties' wallets, we have no
mechanism to take them back. The sponsor and uplines who received
their commissions did nothing wrong — they earned them under the
plan you bought into. Asking them to return the money so you can have
a refund would be unfair to them.

For this reason, **purchases on SuperAdPro are final**. You should
only buy if you are sure you want what you are buying. We strongly
encourage members to:

- Read the documentation for any product or tier before buying.
- Try the free / lower-priced features first.
- Reach out via support if you have questions, **before** you click
  Agree & Proceed.

## 4. When we WILL refund you

We will refund you in the following situations regardless of section 3:

- **Platform fault.** Your payment confirmed but our system failed to
  activate the product (e.g. the activation cron didn't fire, your
  Grid slot wasn't created, your AI credits weren't credited). We
  consider this our fault and we'll fix it — including refunding if
  the activation cannot be completed.

- **Duplicate purchase.** You were charged twice for the same
  transaction because of a technical issue (double-click, network
  retry, payment processor glitch). We use idempotency safeguards to
  prevent this, but if any duplicate gets through we will refund the
  duplicate.

- **Fraudulent transaction.** Your account was compromised and a
  payment was made without your authorisation, or your payment method
  (card / crypto wallet) was used fraudulently. Notify us as soon as
  you become aware. We will investigate, and if confirmed we will
  refund and reverse any related commission payouts.

- **Where the law requires it.** If applicable consumer protection
  law requires us to refund in a specific situation that we have not
  covered above (for example, if a user is later determined to have
  been a minor or otherwise legally incapable of consenting), we will
  do so.

To request a refund under any of the situations above, contact us at
support@superadpro.com with your username, the transaction details,
and a description of what happened. We will respond within 5 working
days.

crypto exchange after consenting to these terms, we will:

- Submit our records of your consent to immediate activation, our
  delivery of the product / service, and the immediate commission
  flows that resulted from your purchase.
- Cooperate fully with any investigation by the card issuer or
  payment processor.
- Reserve the right to suspend your account pending the outcome and
  to deduct any successfully clawed-back amounts from any future
  earnings or balance you accrue on the platform.

Where commissions have already been paid to your sponsor or upline as
a result of your purchase, and your purchase is later reversed by
chargeback, the related commissions may be deducted from those
recipients' accounts. By making a purchase you are agreeing that this
is fair — your sponsor only earned the commission because your
payment was real and final.

guarantee any level of income**. Your earnings depend entirely on

activity of your downline, and many factors outside our control.

Past testimonials and example earnings figures shown on the
platform reflect specific members' results and **are not a promise
