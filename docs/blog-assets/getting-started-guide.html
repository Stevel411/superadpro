Blog Help

Start your blog & share it with the world

From nothing to published in a few minutes — no tech skills needed.

1

Create your blog

In your dashboard, open Blog and click “Launch my blog.” That's it — your blog appears instantly, with a ready-made design and its own web address. Add a name and tagline if you like.

2

Write your first post

Click New post, add a title, and write your story. Drag in photos, or paste a video link (YouTube, Vimeo, Loom) to embed it. Everything saves automatically, so you can stop and come back anytime.

3

Publish it to the world

When you're happy, click Publish. Your post goes live on the internet straight away — anyone can read and share it. Copy your link and post it on social, in emails, or in your bio.

Optional — when you're ready
◆

Make it yours

Open Appearance to pick a theme and font pairing. Switch anytime — your posts never change.

☉

Use your own web address

In Settings › Domain, type your domain and copy one record. We connect it and switch on HTTPS for you — no code.

Every blog keeps a small “Powered by SuperAdPro” link in the footer. When a reader clicks it and joins, they join under you.
