Refund Policy

How refunds work at SuperAdPro

Last updated: 23 May 2026
SuperAdPro is a commission-based platform. When you pay for a membership, course, or other product, a large portion of your payment is automatically distributed to other members as commissions. These payments cannot be reclaimed. This page explains exactly what we can and cannot refund.

The basic principle

On most SaaS platforms, your money sits with the company until you ask for a refund. SuperAdPro is different — when you pay, commissions flow immediately to your sponsor and (depending on the product) to other members in the network.

Refunds are therefore limited to the portion the company retains after commissions are paid. We cannot refund money that has already left the company and gone to other members.

The 7-day refund window

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of payment. After 7 days, all payments are considered final and non-refundable.

We chose 7 days because it gives you enough time to evaluate whether SuperAdPro is right for you, while preventing the manipulation of our commission system through long-delayed refund requests.

How to request a refund

If you're eligible for a refund (within the 7-day window):

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to Account → Billing
  • Find the payment you want refunded
  • Click "Request refund"

Refunds are processed automatically. Your card statement will show the refund within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Subscription cancellation (separate from refunds)

You can cancel your membership subscription at any time from your account. Cancellation stops future charges but does not refund past payments. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

⚠ Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your card issuer (a "chargeback") instead of requesting a refund through us first, your account will be immediately suspended. Any earned commissions in your wallet will be forfeited and any active subscription will be cancelled.

We accept chargebacks as a last resort if our refund process has failed you, but please contact us first via support@superadpro.com before filing a card dispute.

Crypto payments

For payments made via USDT on BSC (Binance Smart Chain), refunds follow the same partial-refund rules above but are sent as USDT to a wallet address you provide. Network fees may be deducted from the refund.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear, or you need help with a specific situation, email us at support@superadpro.com. We try to respond within 24 hours on business days.
