Refund Policy

How refunds work at AdvantageLife

Last updated: 16 July 2026
There are only two things you can pay for on AdvantageLife, and they work completely differently. Your lifetime membership is paid to us, so we can refund it in full for 7 days — no conditions. Campaign packs are paid member-to-member and never pass through us, so we hold nothing to refund. This page explains both, plainly.

The basic principle

Whether we can refund something depends entirely on whether we ever held your money.

The $100 lifetime membership is paid to us. Nobody earns a commission on it — not your sponsor, not anyone. Because we hold all of it, we can refund all of it. If you change your mind within 7 days, you get 100% back. No questions, no partial figure, no deductions.

Campaign packs are different, and you should understand this before you buy one. A pack is bought directly from another member: your payment goes from your wallet to theirs. It never touches AdvantageLife. We take no percentage and hold no part of it. That means we have nothing to refund from — all pack purchases are final.

What can and can't be refunded

Product You pay Refundable
Lifetime membership $100 once — paid to us 100% within 7 days. Nobody earns commission on your join, so we hold the full amount and return the full amount.
Campaign pack $10–$1,000 — paid to another member Not refundable. The money goes wallet-to-wallet to the selling member and never passes through AdvantageLife. We cannot refund what we never receive. This is shown to you at the point of sale, before you pay.
There is no monthly membership, subscription or renewal. AdvantageLife charges $100 once. Nothing recurs, so there is nothing to cancel.

The 7-day refund window

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of payment. After 7 days, all payments are considered final and non-refundable.

Seven days is long enough to look around properly, open every tool and decide it isn't for you. The membership is the only thing we can refund, and within that window we refund it in full — we don't ask why.

How to request a refund

If you're eligible for a refund (within the 7-day window):

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to Account → Billing
  • Find the payment you want refunded
  • Click "Request refund"

Refunds are processed automatically. Your card statement will show the refund within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Nothing to cancel

AdvantageLife has no subscription. You pay $100 once and your access never expires, so there is no recurring charge to stop and no billing period to run out. After the 7-day window your membership simply continues, for life.

⚠ Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your card issuer (a "chargeback") instead of requesting a refund through us first, your account will be immediately suspended and your lifetime access revoked. Note that a chargeback cannot recover a Campaign pack payment: that money went directly to another member and was never held by us.

We accept chargebacks as a last resort if our refund process has failed you, but please contact us first via support@advantagelife.club before filing a card dispute.

Crypto payments

If you paid your $100 membership in USDT on BSC (Binance Smart Chain), a 7-day refund is returned as USDT to a wallet address you provide. Network fees may be deducted from the refund. Pack payments are member-to-member and are not refundable regardless of how they were paid.
