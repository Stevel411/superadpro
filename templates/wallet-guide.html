1

Common questions
What's the difference between USDT and USDC?
Both are "stablecoins" worth $1 each. USDT (Tether) is the most widely used. USDC (USD Coin) is issued by Circle. SuperAdPro accepts both — use whichever your exchange provides.
Can I use Coinbase to send payments?
We recommend MetaMask or Trust Wallet. If you buy crypto on Coinbase, withdraw it to your MetaMask wallet first, then pay from there. This ensures your payment is detected correctly.
What if I send on the wrong network?
Funds sent on the wrong network cannot be recovered. Always verify the network is Polygon before sending. Send a small test amount first.
How do I receive my commissions?
Commissions credit to your SuperAdPro balance instantly. When you withdraw, USDT is sent directly to your MetaMask wallet on Polygon.
Is my money safe in MetaMask?
Yes. MetaMask is self-custody — only YOU control your funds. No company, including SuperAdPro, can access your wallet. Over 100 million people use it worldwide.
How much do I need to get started?
Basic membership is $20/month in USDT or USDC, plus about $0.50 of POL for gas. Your wallet is free. Total to start: roughly $21.
I've never used crypto — is this hard?
Setting up MetaMask takes 5 minutes. Sending crypto is like a bank transfer but faster and cheaper. Once set up, you won't need to think about it again.
