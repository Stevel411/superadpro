Setting up MetaMask

1
Visit metamask.io and download the browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) or mobile app (iOS, Android).
2
Click "Create a new wallet" and set a strong password.
3
Write down your 12-word recovery phrase on paper and store it somewhere safe. This is the ONLY way to recover your wallet if you lose access. Never share it with anyone.
4
Confirm the recovery phrase and your wallet is ready. Your wallet address starts with 0x — this is what you'll use for payments.
⚠️
Never share your recovery phrase. Not with SuperAdPro, not with anyone. Anyone who has your 12 words can access your funds. SuperAdPro will never ask for your recovery phrase.
2
Add the Polygon network

Why Polygon?

Sending $20 on Ethereum costs $5-15 in fees. On Polygon, the same transfer costs less than $0.01. Same security, 1000x cheaper.

Adding Polygon to MetaMask

1
Open MetaMask and click the network dropdown at the top (it usually says "Ethereum Mainnet").
2
Click "Add network" and search for "Polygon".
3
Select Polygon Mainnet and click "Approve" then "Switch to network".
4
Done! MetaMask now shows Polygon. Your wallet address is the same on Polygon as on Ethereum — you don't need a new one.
💡
Polygon is already built into MetaMask on most versions. You may just need to select it from the network list — no manual setup needed.

Buying on an exchange (most common)

1
Create an account on Binance, Kraken, or any major exchange. Complete their identity verification.
2
Deposit funds via bank transfer or card, then buy USDT or USDC.
3
Click "Withdraw", paste your MetaMask wallet address, and select Polygon as the network. This is critical — choosing the wrong network means lost funds.
4
Confirm the withdrawal. Your USDT/USDC will arrive in MetaMask within a few minutes.
⚠️
Always select "Polygon" as the withdrawal network. If you send on Ethereum, Solana, or any other network, the funds will not arrive in your SuperAdPro-compatible wallet. Start with a small test amount first.
💡
You'll also need a tiny amount of POL (Polygon's native token) for transaction fees — about $0.50 worth is enough for hundreds of transactions. Most exchanges let you withdraw POL on Polygon too.
Common questions
What's the difference between USDT and USDC?
Both are "stablecoins" — digital tokens always worth $1. USDT (Tether) is the most widely used, USDC (USD Coin) is issued by Circle/Coinbase. SuperAdPro accepts both. Use whichever your exchange gives you.
Can I use Coinbase to send payments?
We recommend using MetaMask or Trust Wallet instead. Coinbase's exchange sends from internal wallets which can cause payment matching issues. If you buy crypto on Coinbase, withdraw it to your MetaMask wallet first, then pay from there.
What if I send on the wrong network?
Unfortunately, sending on the wrong network (like Ethereum instead of Polygon) means the funds won't arrive and cannot be recovered. Always double-check the network is set to Polygon before sending. We recommend sending a small test amount first.
How do I receive my commissions?
Commissions are credited to your SuperAdPro balance. When you're ready to withdraw, go to your Wallet page and request a withdrawal. Your earnings are sent as USDT on Polygon directly to the wallet address in your account settings.
Is my money safe in MetaMask?
Yes — MetaMask is a self-custody wallet, meaning only YOU control your funds. No company, including MetaMask and SuperAdPro, can access your wallet. The key is keeping your 12-word recovery phrase safe and private.
How much do I need to get started?
A Basic membership is $20/month in USDT or USDC, plus about $0.50 worth of POL for transaction fees. That's it. Your wallet setup is completely free.
I've never used crypto before — is this complicated?
It's simpler than it sounds. Setting up MetaMask takes about 5 minutes — similar to creating any online account. Buying and sending USDT is like a bank transfer but faster and cheaper. Once set up, you won't need to think about it again.
