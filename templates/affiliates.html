Stream 3 — Course Sales
Course Marketplace — Infinite Pass-Up System
🎓 100% Commission + Pass-Ups
You Keep Most Sales. Three Pass-Ups Build Your Upline.
Sell premium marketing courses priced at $100, $300, and $500. You keep 100% commission on most sales. Only your 2nd, 4th, and 6th sales pass up to your sponsor — just three pass-ups total.
