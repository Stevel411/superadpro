üöÄGetting Started
What is SuperAdPro? ‚åÑ
SuperAdPro is a video advertising platform where advertisers buy engaged video views, marketers launch AI-optimised campaigns, and affiliates earn commissions by growing the network. All payments are made in USDT on the Base blockchain.
Is it free to register? ‚åÑ
Yes ‚Äî registration is completely free. As a free member you can explore the platform, receive your personal referral link and refer others. Your first paying referral automatically activates your $20 membership for month one ‚Äî meaning the platform pays your entry for you. From month two onwards, your $20 membership renews automatically from your wallet balance, which you build up through Watch-to-Earn commissions, grid earnings and Stream 1 referral income.
What do I need to get started? ‚åÑ
You need: (1) a valid email address, (2) a Base Chain compatible crypto wallet (such as MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet) with USDT, and (3) a beta access code if you're joining during our beta phase. That's it.
Do I need crypto experience to use SuperAdPro? ‚åÑ
Basic experience is helpful ‚Äî you'll need to be able to send USDT from a crypto wallet. If you've never used crypto before, our support team can walk you through setting up a wallet and making your first transfer. Visit our Support page for guidance.
What is a beta access code? ‚åÑ
During our beta launch phase, registration requires a beta access code. Codes are distributed by existing members and the SuperAdPro team. If you don't have one, contact us via the Support page or ask the person who referred you.
üé¨Advertising
What are engaged video views? ‚åÑ
Engaged video views come from active SuperAdPro members who watch your video content as part of their earning activity. Every view is from a real person participating in the platform ‚Äî not bots, not passive impressions. The maximum number of views your campaign can receive is set by your chosen campaign tier, subject to active membership levels at the time of your campaign.
What video formats are accepted? ‚åÑ
We accept MP4 video files. For best results, we recommend: 1920√ó1080 (Full HD) resolution, H.264 encoding, under 500MB file size, and between 15 seconds and 5 minutes in length. Shorter, punchy ads typically perform best.
How quickly does my campaign go live? ‚åÑ
Once your tier payment is confirmed on-chain (usually under 30 seconds), your campaign is activated. After uploading your video and setting your audience preferences, delivery begins within 24 hours.
Can I run multiple campaigns at the same time? ‚åÑ
Yes ‚Äî from the Builder tier ($50) and above, you can run multiple campaigns simultaneously. Each tier activation creates a separate campaign slot. Activate multiple tiers to scale your advertising reach.
How does AI optimisation work? ‚åÑ
Our AI analyses your video content, identifies the most relevant audience segments, and continuously adjusts delivery to maximise engagement. It optimises for view completion rate, click-through rate, and audience relevance ‚Äî running 24/7 throughout your campaign without any manual input from you.
What targeting options are available? ‚åÑ
Basic targeting (interests, geography) is available on all tiers. Advanced targeting ‚Äî including demographics, device type, and behavioural segments ‚Äî is unlocked from the Pro tier ($100) upwards. The AI then fine-tunes delivery within your selected parameters.
‚óàAffiliate Programme & Grid
Do I have to participate in the affiliate programme? ‚åÑ
No ‚Äî affiliate participation is completely optional. You can use SuperAdPro purely as an advertising platform without ever sharing your referral link. The affiliate programme is there if you want to earn by growing the network, but it's not required.
How much can I earn as an affiliate? ‚åÑ
There is no cap. You earn 25% direct sponsor commission on every personal referral's entry, plus 8.75% uni-level commission from every seat across all 8 levels of your grid. As your network grows, your recurring income compounds automatically.
How does the 8√ó8 Profit Engine Grid work? ‚åÑ
Your 8√ó8 grid has 63 seats across 8 levels. Every new member who joins through your network ‚Äî whether referred by you directly or by your downline ‚Äî fills a seat. You earn 8.75% uni-level commission on every seat fill, plus 25% direct sponsor commission on anyone you personally referred. When all 63 seats fill, the grid auto-advances and starts a new advance.
What is the difference between direct sponsor commission and uni-level commission? ‚åÑ
Direct sponsor commission (25%) is paid to the person who personally referred the new member. Uni-level commission (8.75% per level) is paid to each of the 8 sponsors above the new member in the chain ‚Äî whether they referred them directly or not. The vast majority of every entry is distributed directly to the affiliate network, with a small management fee retained to cover platform operations.
Do I need a referral to join? ‚åÑ
No ‚Äî a sponsor is optional. If you weren't referred by anyone, simply leave the sponsor field blank during registration. You'll still be placed in the network and benefit from upline spillover filling your grid.
Can I activate more than one tier to increase earnings? ‚åÑ
Yes ‚Äî activating multiple tiers creates additional grid positions and unlocks more advertising capacity. Each tier runs its own independent 8√ó8 grid, multiplying your earning potential.
üí∞Payments & USDT
Why does SuperAdPro use USDT? ‚åÑ
USDT (Tether) is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar ‚Äî so 1 USDT always equals $1. This removes volatility risk and makes payouts predictable. We use the Base blockchain for transactions because it's fast (seconds) and cheap (pennies in gas fees).
Which wallets are supported? ‚åÑ
Any wallet that supports USDT on the Base network will work. Popular options include MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and Trust Wallet. Make sure you're using the Base network ‚Äî not Ethereum mainnet ‚Äî to avoid sending funds to the wrong chain.
How quickly are commissions paid out? ‚åÑ
Commissions are paid instantly ‚Äî the moment a qualifying tier activation occurs, your USDT is sent directly to your registered wallet address. There are no minimum withdrawal thresholds or waiting periods.
Are there any withdrawal fees? ‚åÑ
SuperAdPro does not charge withdrawal fees. The minimum withdrawal amount is $5 USDT. You will pay a small Base Chain network gas fee when transactions are processed ‚Äî typically less than $0.10 per transaction. This is paid by the platform on your behalf for commission payouts.
What if I send USDT on the wrong network? ‚åÑ
Important: Always send USDT on the Base network only. Sending on Ethereum mainnet or another chain will result in funds not being received and may not be recoverable. Double-check your network selection before every transaction. Contact support immediately if you believe you've sent on the wrong network.
Can I change my wallet address after registration? ‚åÑ
Yes ‚Äî you can update your wallet address from the Profile section of your dashboard. For security purposes, wallet address changes may require identity verification. All future commissions will be sent to your updated address.
üë§Account & Security
How do I reset my password? ‚åÑ
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact support.
Can I have more than one account? ‚åÑ
No ‚Äî each person may only hold one account on SuperAdPro. Multiple accounts from the same individual are against our Terms of Service and may result in all associated accounts being suspended. If you need to manage multiple campaigns, you can activate multiple tiers under one account.
Is my personal data secure? ‚åÑ
Yes. All passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS encryption across the platform and do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I close my account? ‚åÑ
To close your account, contact our support team via the Support page. Please note that any pending commissions will be paid to your registered wallet before closure, and your grid position will be removed. Active campaigns cannot be refunded once activated.
