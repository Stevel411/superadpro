Frequently Asked Questions

What is SuperAdPro?

SuperAdPro is a video-based affiliate platform for network marketers and affiliates. You pay $10/month for unlimited access to high-quality marketing training videos and earn 100% recurring commissions when you refer others.

How do I earn money?

You earn 100% of the $10 monthly subscription fee from every person you refer. You can also activate matrix tiers for additional leveraged earnings from spillover and matching bonuses.

What is the daily video requirement?

You must watch the required number of 5-minute videos every day (30 seconds minimum each) to stay qualified for commissions and matrix earnings.

Is this a pyramid scheme?

No. SuperAdPro is a legitimate affiliate platform with real educational video content. Rewards are performance-based and tied to active video watching and referrals.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Earnings are paid in USDT on the Base chain. You can withdraw directly to your crypto wallet at any time with no minimum threshold.
