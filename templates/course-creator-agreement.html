Course Creator Agreement

Last updated: March 2026

This Course Creator Agreement ("Agreement") governs the creation, publication, and sale of courses on the SuperAdPro platform ("Platform") by registered Pro members ("Creators"). By creating a course on SuperAdPro, you agree to be bound by the terms set forth below.

1. Eligibility

Only members with an active Pro membership ($30/month) are eligible to create and publish courses on the SuperAdPro marketplace. Basic members may upgrade to Pro at any time to access course creation features.

2. Content Ownership & Rights

  1. Your Content: You warrant and represent that you own or have obtained all necessary rights, licences, and permissions for all content you upload to the Platform, including but not limited to text, video, images, audio, and PDF materials.
  2. Licence to SuperAdPro: By publishing a course, you grant SuperAdPro a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence to display, distribute, promote, and sell your course on the Platform and in marketing materials, including social media, email newsletters, and advertisements.
  3. No Transfer of Ownership: This licence does not transfer ownership of your content. You retain all intellectual property rights to your course materials.

3. Commission Structure

The commission split on every course sale is fixed and non-negotiable:

  1. 50% to Creator: Credited to your SuperAdPro wallet immediately upon confirmed purchase.
  2. 25% to Creator's Sponsor: Credited to the wallet of the member who recruited you to SuperAdPro.
  3. 25% to SuperAdPro: Platform fee covering hosting, bandwidth, AI review, and operational costs.

You set the price for your course (minimum $25 USD). Commissions are calculated on the full sale price.

4. Course Review Process

All courses undergo a multi-layer review before publication:

  1. Automated Quality Check: Verifies minimum structural requirements (lesson count, duration, content length, thumbnail).
  2. AI Content Scan: Scans for plagiarism, prohibited content, misleading claims, and overall quality.
  3. Admin Review: Human review to assess quality, coherence, and educational value.

SuperAdPro reserves the right to reject any course that does not meet quality standards, with or without reason. Rejected courses receive feedback to help the Creator improve and resubmit.

5. Prohibited Content

Courses must not contain:

  1. Plagiarised or copyright-infringing material
  2. Hate speech, discrimination, or content promoting violence
  3. Explicit sexual content or nudity
  4. Illegal activity or instructions for illegal activity
  5. Misleading income or earnings claims (e.g., "guaranteed income", "get rich quick")
  6. Unsubstantiated health, medical, or financial claims
  7. Content that exists solely to promote a product or service without genuine educational value
  8. AI-generated content without substantial human editing and original insight

6. Platform Role & Liability

  1. Hosting Platform: SuperAdPro acts as a hosting platform and marketplace. SuperAdPro is not a publisher and does not endorse, verify, or guarantee the accuracy of any member-created course content.
  2. No Liability: SuperAdPro disclaims all liability for claims, damages, or losses arising from course content created by members.
  3. Right to Remove: SuperAdPro reserves the right to remove, unpublish, or suspend any course at any time, with or without notice, at its sole discretion.

7. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless SuperAdPro, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including legal fees) arising out of or related to your course content, including but not limited to copyright infringement, trademark violations, or any other intellectual property claims.

8. Refund Policy

  1. Buyers may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
  2. On refund, all commissions are reversed: Creator, Sponsor, and Company shares are debited from respective wallets.
  3. Refund requests beyond 7 days are at SuperAdPro's discretion.

9. Course Removal by Creator

  1. You may unpublish or remove your course from the marketplace at any time.
  2. Existing buyers who purchased your course before removal retain lifetime access to the content they purchased.
  3. Revenue and commission records are preserved permanently for accounting and audit purposes.

10. Account Suspension

  1. If your SuperAdPro account is suspended for any reason, all your courses will be automatically unpublished.
  2. Existing buyers retain access to purchased content.
  3. No new sales will occur during suspension.

11. DMCA Compliance

  1. SuperAdPro will promptly remove any content upon receipt of a valid DMCA takedown notice.
  2. Creators may submit a counter-notification if they believe content was wrongly removed.
  3. Repeat infringers (2 or more valid DMCA claims) will have course creation privileges permanently revoked.

12. Modifications

SuperAdPro may update this Agreement at any time. Continued use of the course creation features after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. Material changes will be communicated via notification.

13. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with applicable laws. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
