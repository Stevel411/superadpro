7. The 3/6/9 pass-up

You keep sales 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 8 and every sale from the 10th onward. Your 3rd, 6th and 9th sales pass up to the first qualified member above you in your pass-up chain.

  • A direct sale where the recipient is not qualified goes to the company. It does not climb.
  • A pass-up climbs the chain until it reaches a qualified member. Only if nobody in the chain qualifies does it go to the company.
  • The sale counter increments when a sale is confirmed, not when it is started.

Commission rules may change with reasonable notice. Changes are not applied retrospectively to sales already confirmed.
