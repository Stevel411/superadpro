How the Spillover Grid Works
1
When anyone in your network purchases this tier, they fill a seat in your grid — not just your personal referrals.
2
Colours show the depth: cyan = your direct referrals, green = their referrals, purple = level 3, and so on. Hover any seat to see who's there.
3
Each person fills one seat per grid per advance. You need 64 unique network members at this tier to complete.
4
When all 64 seats fill, a new advance auto-starts and the cycle repeats. Your grid grows as your network grows.
5
Commissions follow the sponsor chain — 40% to direct sponsor, 6.875% uni-level to 8 levels up, 5% platform. The grid is your visual progress tracker.
