Frequently Asked
Advertiser Questions
What video formats do you accept?
We accept MP4, MOV, and WebM. Recommended resolution is 1080p (1920x1080) for best results. Maximum file size is 100MB per video.
How quickly will my campaign go live?
Most campaigns go live within 24 hours of payment. Once your USDT payment is confirmed on Base Chain, your video enters the viewing queue automatically.
Can I target specific audiences?
Higher tiers (Pro and above) include advanced targeting options including interest categories, geographic regions, and demographic filters. AI optimisation is included on all tiers.
How do I track performance?
Your campaign dashboard shows real-time views, completion rates, and engagement metrics. You can see exactly how your video is performing at any time.
Are the views from real people?
Yes — every single view comes from an active, verified SuperAdPro member. Zero bots, zero click farms. Our members watch your video as part of their daily earning activity.
Can I run multiple campaigns?
Yes — Builder tier and above support multiple simultaneous campaigns. You can run different videos for different products or audiences at the same time.
