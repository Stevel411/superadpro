What is a Short Link?
A short link is a condensed, branded URL that redirects visitors to any destination. ProTrack short links do far more than shorten — every click is logged with the visitor's country, device, browser and traffic source in real time. Set click caps, expiry dates, geo-based redirects and mobile/desktop split destinations.

What is a Link Rotator?
A link rotator takes a single URL and automatically distributes visitors across multiple destination URLs. Perfect for split-testing landing pages, spreading traffic across offers, or sharing load between campaigns. Choose equal distribution or assign custom weights to send more traffic to your best-performing pages.
✦ Equal or weighted split✦ Unlimited destinations✦ Single shareable URL✦ Real-time distribution

What is a UTM Builder?
UTM parameters are tracking tags you append to any URL so analytics platforms like Google Analytics can identify exactly where your traffic came from. The ProTrack UTM Builder generates these tags instantly — define your source (e.g. Facebook), medium (e.g. email), campaign name, and content variant, then copy or shorten the result in one click.
✦ Source & medium tags✦ Campaign & content tracking✦ Google Analytics ready✦ One-click shorten
