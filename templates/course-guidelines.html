Course Quality Guidelines

These standards ensure every course on the SuperAdPro marketplace delivers real value. Read these before creating your first course — they're the bar your content needs to meet.

Structure Requirements

Minimum 3 lessons per course
Your course must have at least 3 lessons spread across at least 1 chapter. This ensures students get substantive content.
Minimum 10 minutes total content
The combined duration of all lessons must be at least 10 minutes. Short, focused courses are fine — but they need substance.
At least 1 free preview lesson
Buyers should be able to sample your teaching style before purchasing. Mark at least one lesson as a free preview.
Text lessons need 100+ words each
If your lesson is text-based, it must contain at least 100 words of original content. One-liners don't count as lessons.

Content Requirements

Title: 10–100 characters
Descriptive and specific. No ALL CAPS. Tell buyers exactly what they'll learn.
Description: 100+ characters
Explain what students will learn, who the course is for, and what outcomes they can expect. Detailed descriptions convert better.
Thumbnail: required
Upload a course cover image (minimum 600×400 pixels, JPG or PNG). No text-heavy images — keep it clean and visual.
Price: minimum $25
Courses must be priced at $25 or above. This ensures the content is valued and the commission split is meaningful.

Prohibited Content

Courses containing any of the following will be rejected:

  • Plagiarised or copyright-infringing material
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or content promoting violence
  • Explicit sexual content or nudity
  • Illegal activity or instructions for illegal activity
  • Misleading income claims: "guaranteed income", "get rich quick", unsubstantiated earnings promises
  • Unsubstantiated health or medical claims
  • Content that exists solely to promote a product/service without genuine educational value
  • AI-generated content without substantial human editing and original insight

Video Quality

  • Audio must be clear and free of excessive background noise
  • Video resolution minimum 720p (1280×720)
  • No watermarks from other platforms
  • Screen recordings must have legible, readable text

The Review Process

Every course goes through a three-layer review before going live:

Layer 1: Automated Quality Check

Instantly checks structure requirements — lesson count, duration, word count, thumbnail, description length, and pricing. If any check fails, you'll see exactly what needs fixing.

Layer 2: AI Content Scan

Our AI scans your text content for plagiarism, prohibited content, misleading claims, and overall quality. Courses with plagiarism scores above 40% or high-severity issues are auto-rejected with specific feedback.

Layer 3: Admin Review

Courses that pass the AI scan are reviewed by a human admin who checks the overall quality, coherence, and value of the course. Approved courses go live immediately on the marketplace.
