# SuperAdPro — Profit Projections
**Last updated: 3 April 2026**

> These projections cover current revenue streams only. Not included: Course Marketplace (100% commission pass-up cascade), Video Creator service, future premium tools, and additional AI products. Actual profits will be higher once these streams are live.

---

## Assumptions

- 70% Basic ($20/mo), 30% upgrade to Pro ($35/mo)
- All members activate T1 ($20), T2 ($50), T3 ($100)
- 40% also activate T4 ($200)
- 20% buy SuperScene Starter ($11), 10% Creator ($33), 5% Studio ($110)
- 15% buy Email Boost 1K ($5), 5% buy 5K ($19)
- 10% use Pay It Forward ($20 gift)
