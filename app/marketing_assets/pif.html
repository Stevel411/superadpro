Common questions.

If yours isn't here, ask us.

What if they never claim the gift?

The voucher sits in your "available" stack until claimed or until you let it expire. No commission is paid until activation. You can have up to 10 unclaimed vouchers at a time.

Can they pay it forward too?

Yes. Once they're active, they can gift a membership to someone else exactly the same way. We track chain depth so you can see how far your original gift travelled.

How do I send the gift link?

Once you create a voucher, you get a unique URL like superadpro.com/gift/SAPGIFT-X7K2-9PQR. Share via WhatsApp, text, email, DM — anywhere. On mobile, the native share sheet lets you send to any installed app.

Can the recipient use the gift if they already have an account?

Only if their account is currently inactive (Free tier). Gifts are designed to activate new and inactive members. Existing active members can't claim a gift on their own account, but they can forward the link to someone who's eligible.

Will they know who gifted it?

Yes. Your name appears on the gift landing page and stays visible on their dashboard after they activate — including any personal message you wrote.

Are there refunds?

Gifts paid by card are refundable for 7 days, only if unclaimed. Once a gift is claimed, the membership is activated and the commission is paid — refunds aren't possible from that point. Crypto-paid gifts are non-refundable per platform policy (USDT is final).

Who would you give a free month to?

Someone scrolling past a tool they can't afford. Someone you want to bring into your network. Someone who'd do it for you.
