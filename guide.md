# SuperAdPro — Watch to Earn & Campaign Targeting Guide

## How Watch to Earn Works

Watch to Earn is the engine of SuperAdPro. Members watch video campaigns uploaded by advertisers, and in return, their commissions stay qualified. Advertisers get real, targeted views from real people — not bots.

Every member has a **daily video quota** based on their Campaign Tier level. Watching your quota each day keeps your income streams active: membership commissions, Income Grid earnings, SuperScene usage commissions, and course marketplace earnings all depend on staying qualified.

### Daily Quota by Tier

| Tier | Name | Daily Videos | Monthly Views Delivered |
|------|------|-------------|----------------------|
| 1 | Starter | 1 | 500 |
| 2 | Builder | 2 | 1,500 |
| 3 | Pro | 3 | 5,000 |
| 4 | Advanced | 4 | 10,000 |
| 5 | Elite | 5 | 20,000 |
| 6 | Premium | 6 | 30,000 |
| 7 | Executive | 7 | 40,000 |
| 8 | Ultimate | 8 | 50,000 |

If you miss your daily quota for 5 consecutive days, your commission earnings are paused until you resume watching. There's no penalty — just start watching again and your commissions reactivate.

### What Happens When You Watch

Each video plays for 30 seconds. You must keep the tab open and active — the system detects if you switch tabs or pause. Once the timer completes, you mark the video as watched. The system then immediately serves you the next video from the smart rotation queue.

Videos rotate automatically. Even if only a few campaigns exist in the system, you'll see them cycle through so you can always complete your daily quota. As more members join and create campaigns, the variety increases naturally.

## How the Smart Targeting Algorithm Works

SuperAdPro doesn't just serve random videos. Every campaign is scored for every watcher using a sophisticated algorithm that considers multiple factors. The highest-scoring campaign is served first.

### The Scoring System

When you open Watch to Earn, the algorithm evaluates every active campaign and assigns it a score based on how relevant it is to you. Here's how each factor contributes:

**Priority Level (Campaign Tier 5+)**
Higher-tier campaigns are served first. An Ultimate tier campaign gets 400 priority points, meaning it reaches viewers before a Starter campaign. This is one of the key benefits of upgrading your campaign tier.

- Tier 5 Elite: +100 points
- Tier 6 Premium: +200 points
- Tier 7 Executive: +300 points
- Tier 8 Ultimate: +400 points

**View Deficit**
Campaigns that are furthest from their view target get priority. A campaign with 10 views out of a 50,000 target scores much higher than one with 45,000 out of 50,000. This ensures fair distribution — every advertiser gets their views delivered.

**Geographic Targeting (Tier 4+ Campaigns)**
If an advertiser targets "United Kingdom" and you've set your country to United Kingdom in your profile, that campaign gets +30 points for you. If you're in a different country, it gets -20 points. The campaign is still shown to you eventually — it's just deprioritised in favour of better matches.

**Interest Targeting (Tier 4+ Campaigns)**
Advertisers can set target interests like "crypto, marketing, fitness." If your profile interests include "crypto," you get +15 points per matching interest. No overlap gives a -10 penalty. The more interests you add to your profile, the better the algorithm can match you.

**Demographics (Tier 4+ Campaigns)**
Advertisers can target by gender and age range. A match gives +20 points, a mismatch gives -15 points. Setting your gender and age range in your profile helps the algorithm serve you the most relevant content.

**Featured & Spotlight Campaigns (Tier 6+/7+)**
Featured campaigns get +20 points. Spotlight campaigns (Tier 7+) get +40 points. These are premium placements that ensure maximum visibility.

**Freshness Bonus**
Brand new campaigns get up to +21 bonus points in their first week. This ensures new advertisers see immediate results when they launch a campaign.

**Random Tiebreaker**
When multiple campaigns have the same score, the system randomly selects between them. This prevents the same video from playing repeatedly and ensures variety.

### Example: How Scoring Works in Practice

Imagine three active campaigns:

**Campaign A:** Tier 8 Ultimate, targets UK, interests: crypto. Only 500 of 50,000 views delivered.
- Priority: +400
- View deficit: +49 (99% remaining)
- Geo match (you're in UK): +30
- Interest match (you like crypto): +15
- **Total: +494**

**Campaign B:** Tier 2 Builder, no targeting. 1,200 of 1,500 views delivered.
- Priority: 0
- View deficit: +10 (20% remaining)
- No targeting bonuses
- **Total: +10**

**Campaign C:** Tier 5 Elite, targets US, interests: fitness. 5,000 of 20,000 views delivered.
- Priority: +100
- View deficit: +37 (75% remaining)
- Geo mismatch (you're in UK, targets US): -20
- Interest mismatch (you like crypto, not fitness): -10
- **Total: +107**

**Result:** You see Campaign A first, then C, then B. Campaign A's UK crypto audience member is exactly who the advertiser wanted to reach.

## Setting Up Your Profile for Better Targeting

Go to **Account** and fill in your targeting profile:

**Country** — Select your country. This is the most important field. Advertisers targeting your country will have their campaigns served to you first.

**Gender** — Optional. Set this if you want to see campaigns targeted to your demographic. You can select "Prefer not to say" to opt out.

**Age Range** — Select your age bracket. Advertisers often target specific age groups for their products and services.

**Interests** — This is where the real magic happens. Add your interests separated by commas, for example: *crypto, marketing, fitness, forex, tech, ecommerce*. The more interests you add, the better the algorithm can match you with relevant campaigns. You'll see content that actually matters to you instead of random videos.

## Creating Effective Campaigns (For Advertisers)

When you create a campaign, the targeting options you set directly control who sees your video first.

### Campaign Fields

**Title** — A clear, descriptive title helps you track performance in My Campaigns.

**Video URL** — Paste a YouTube or Vimeo link. The video will be embedded and played to members during their Watch to Earn sessions.

**Category** — Select the closest category for your content. This helps match your campaign to members with related interests.

**Description** — Briefly describe what your video is about. This appears in campaign analytics.

**Target Country** — If your offer is country-specific (e.g. a UK-based service), enter the target country. Members in that country will see your campaign first. Leave empty for worldwide reach.

**Target Interests** — Enter comma-separated interests that describe your ideal viewer: *crypto, marketing, business*. Members with matching interests in their profile will see your campaign first.

### Tips for Maximum Impact

1. **Set targeting fields** — A targeted campaign reaches the right audience faster. Don't leave targeting blank if you know who your ideal viewer is.

2. **Use a compelling video** — Members watch for 30 seconds. Make the first 30 seconds count — hook the viewer immediately.

3. **Upgrade your tier** — Higher tiers get priority queue placement, meaning your campaign is served before lower-tier campaigns. Tier 5+ campaigns always appear first.

4. **Keep campaigns active** — Campaigns run until their view target is met. Higher tiers get more views per cycle.

5. **Use interest tags strategically** — Match your tags to what your ideal customer would put in their profile. If you're promoting a crypto course, use tags like: *crypto, bitcoin, trading, investment, finance*.

## How Views Are Delivered

Views are delivered through the Watch to Earn system. Every active member watches videos as part of their daily qualification. The algorithm ensures:

- **Fair distribution** — Campaigns furthest from their view target get priority
- **Targeted delivery** — Matching profiles are served first
- **Real engagement** — Every view is a real person watching for 30+ seconds
- **No bots** — The system requires active tab presence and timer completion
- **Rotation** — Videos cycle through the pool so every campaign gets exposure

### View Delivery Timeline

View delivery depends on the number of active watchers in the system. With more members watching daily, campaigns reach their view targets faster. Higher-tier campaigns with priority placement complete faster than lower-tier campaigns.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I have to watch videos every day?**
You should try to. Missing 5 consecutive days pauses your commission earnings. But there's no permanent penalty — start watching again and everything reactivates.

**Can I watch my own campaigns?**
The algorithm prioritises other members' campaigns first. Your own campaigns are only shown as a last resort when no other content is available.

**What if there aren't enough videos to hit my quota?**
The system rotates through available campaigns. Even with just a few campaigns in the system, you can complete your quota because videos will cycle.

**Do my profile settings affect what I see?**
Yes. Your country, interests, gender, and age range all influence which campaigns are shown to you first. Fill in your profile for the best experience.

**How do I know my campaign views are real?**
Every view requires 30 seconds of active watching with tab-presence detection. There are no bots, no fake clicks. Each view is logged with the watcher's user ID, timestamp, and duration.

**Can I skip a video I don't like?**
The current system requires you to watch each video for 30 seconds before moving to the next. This ensures advertisers get genuine engagement.

**What happens when my campaign reaches its view target?**
Your campaign becomes inactive and stops receiving views. To get more views, you can create a new campaign or upgrade your tier for a higher monthly view allowance.
